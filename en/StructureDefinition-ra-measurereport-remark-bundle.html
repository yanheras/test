Resource Profile: Risk Adjustment MeasureReport With Remark Bundle

Official URL: http://hl7.org/fhir/us/davinci-ra/StructureDefinition/ra-measurereport-remark-bundle Version: 3.0.0-ballot
Standards status: Trial-use Draft as of 2026-03-08 Maturity Level: 2 Computable Name: RiskAdjustmentMeasureReportRemarkBundle
Other Identifiers: OID:2.16.840.1.113883.4.642.40.29.42.20

Copyright/Legal: Used by permission of HL7 International, all rights reserved Creative Commons License

This bundle contains a Risk Adjustment Coding Gap MeasureReport that has Condition Category Remark (ccRemark) present on at least one of the Condition Categories. It also contains the relevant resources referenced by the ccRemark.

Usages:

  • This Profile is not used by any profiles in this Implementation Guide
